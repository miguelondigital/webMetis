Our Creative Mission

Our mission is to honor, celebrate, and share the living legacy of the Métis culture. This website particularly focuses on the artistry of beadwork. We aim to showcase Métis beadwork by promoting small businesses and museums with beadwork showings. Whether you are reconnecting with your roots or learning for the first time, our space is built to support, inspire and encourage the need to explore culture and appreciate the creativity of human beings.
